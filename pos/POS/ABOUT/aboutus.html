ABOUT US

Our Story

M&M Cafe was born out of a passion for creating exceptional beverages and creating a cozy haven for friends and families to gather. Founded by a loving couple in 2022, our journey began when we embarked on a mission to bring a unique twist to the local milk tea scene. Since then, we've become a cherished part of the community, known not just for our refreshing drinks but for the warmth and hospitality we extend to each and every visitor.
